01 / About

About CIVITAS'26

Driven by innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence, CIVITAS'26 is the flagship civil engineering fest of ConCreate Club, IIT Indore.

Envisioned as The Civil Confluence, the event unites students, academicians, researchers, startups, and industry leaders on one dynamic platform to exchange ideas, showcase innovation, and shape the future of infrastructure.

With the theme "Resilient and Sustainable Infrastructure for Tomorrow," CIVITAS'26 emphasizes environmentally responsible and technologically advanced infrastructure solutions, bridging academic knowledge with real-world challenges.

02 / The Club

ConCreate Club

ConCreate Club is the official civil engineering club under the Science & Technology Council, Gymkhana, IIT Indore.

The club provides a unified platform for students to explore, innovate, compete, and collaborate in civil engineering. It has secured podium finishes for three consecutive years at the Inter IIT Civil Conclave hosted by IIT Roorkee.

How to Register

  1. Visit the official registration portal at https://registration.iiti.ac.in/civitas26/
  2. Read and accept the terms and conditions
  3. Select your event category
  4. Fill in required details: Full Name, College/Organization, Contact, Email, Team Details
  5. Complete payment (including applicable GST)
  6. Enter the correct Transaction ID
  7. Upload payment proof (if required)
  8. Submit and receive confirmation email

Note: Registration is confirmed only after payment verification. For payment issues, email us with your Transaction ID and event details.
